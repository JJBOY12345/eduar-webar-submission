Industry-Oriented AR Education Prototype

EduAR

Explore science concepts in 3D through a marker-based mobile WebAR experience designed for interactive STEM learning.

Project Features

  • Seven STEM learning topics visualized as 3D AR models
  • Marker-based mobile WebAR with no app installation required
  • Topic switching, 3-axis rotation, scaling, reset, and concept details
  • Clean student-friendly interface with guided onboarding

Marker setup

Print or open the Hiro marker on another screen, then point your camera at it after starting the experience.

Open marker image

Current implementation: marker-based WebAR with A-Frame and AR.js. This is a browser prototype, not a native Unity mobile build.
